Teckels are great watchdogs. No need for a smart home when you have a Teckel around! Teckels will let you know when someone is approaching your door before your doorbell knows.

The excellent hearing and sensitive nose of the Teckel give them the tools they need to bolster your home security system.

Equipped with a sharp bark, Teckels can give their owners a heads up when there is anything out of the norm in their environment.
